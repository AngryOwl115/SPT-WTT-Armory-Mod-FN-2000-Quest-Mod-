For those that don't really care about stumbling upon the quest here is the info as follows.
You first get the quest at level 22. 
Quest that starts the chain that leads to labyrinth quests is "Shady Contractor" which leads to "Offensive Reconnaissance" which in hand should lead to multiple quests that give you the labyrinth key card like "Hypothesis testing" or the "The Tarkov Butcher" among other quests from different traders that give access to the labyrinth keycard
In any case in regards to the quest itself. Its pretty straightforward. If you look at the quest photo, the location is in the middle of the labyrinth right where the tractor with candles are, next to the main nuclear bomb room. The weapon and a lore note should both be next to eachother!
